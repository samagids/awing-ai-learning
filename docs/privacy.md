# Awing AI Learning - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 12, 2026

## Our Commitment to Privacy

At Awing AI Learning, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Awing AI Learning is designed to be **offline-first**, meaning you can use the app to learn the Awing language without ever needing to create an account or connect to the internet. All core learning features work without any data collection.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Voluntarily

**Google Sign-In (Optional):** If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information. This is used only to:

- Create and manage your user profile within the app
- Sync your learning progress and settings across multiple devices using Firebase Firestore
- Enable the optional teacher exam functionality

Signing in with Google is completely optional. The app functions fully offline without it.

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Anonymous Analytics (Optional):** If you enable anonymous usage analytics (opt-in setting), we collect:

**Activity Events:**
- Lessons opened
- Quiz attempts and scores
- Features used

**Device Info:**
- Unique anonymous device ID
- App version
- Timestamp of events

This data is **completely anonymous** — no personal identifying information is collected. You can opt out of analytics at any time in Settings > Feedback.

### 3. Contribution System Data

If you use the Contribute feature to suggest corrections or new vocabulary:

- Your submission (text and optional audio) is stored locally on your device
- When you share a contribution, it is sent (via email, WhatsApp, or other platform) to the developer for review
- The developer reviews, approves, or rejects contributions before applying them to future app versions
- You retain full control over sharing your contributions

---

## Cloud Data Storage (Firebase Firestore)

### How We Use Your Data

If you sign in with Google, your learning progress and settings are stored in Firebase Firestore (Google's cloud database) to enable:

- **Cross-device sync:** Your progress automatically syncs when you use the app on different devices
- **Progress backup:** Your learning history, quizzes, achievements, and preferences are backed up
- **Exam functionality:** Teachers can create and monitor exams for students

### Data Security

Firebase Firestore data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Access is controlled by Firebase security rules that ensure:

- Only you can read and write your own data
- Data is never visible to other users
- Google staff cannot access your personal data

### Data Retention

Your cloud data persists as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of all cloud data at any time by contacting samagids@gmail.com.

## Data Security

### How We Protect Your Information

- **Encryption in transit:** All data sent to/from Firebase uses HTTPS encryption
- **Encryption at rest:** Cloud data is encrypted by Google's infrastructure
- **Offline-first design:** Most app data stays on your device and is never transmitted
- **Secure authentication:** Google Sign-In uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0
- **No insecure storage:** Passwords and sensitive tokens are never stored unencrypted

### What We Cannot Guarantee

While we implement best practices, no method of data transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to:

- Use strong passwords for your Google account
- Keep your device's operating system and apps updated
- Be cautious when using the app on public Wi-Fi networks
