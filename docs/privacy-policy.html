Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Awing AI Learning ("the App") is developed by Dr. Guidion Sama, DIT. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

The App is designed with privacy in mind, especially since it is used by children. We collect minimal information:

Google Sign-In: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. This information is stored locally on your device to manage your account and profiles. We do not store your Google credentials.

Learning Progress: Your lesson progress, quiz scores, achievement badges, and vocabulary review data are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data is not transmitted to any server.

Speech Data: When you use the pronunciation practice feature, audio is processed on your device using the operating system's built-in speech recognition. Audio recordings are not stored or transmitted.

Optional Cloud Backup: If you choose to enable cloud backup, your progress data is saved to your personal Google Drive in the app-specific data folder. Only you can access this data through your Google account.

Optional Analytics: If enabled, anonymous usage events (lesson completions, quiz scores) may be sent to help improve the App. No personally identifiable information is included. You can opt out in your profile settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for children and complies with applicable children's privacy laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is described above. Parent/guardian features (PIN protection, parental gate) are built into the App to provide parental oversight.

How We Use Information

Information is used solely to provide the App's features: managing user profiles, tracking learning progress, and enabling cloud backup. We do not sell, share, or rent your information to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. Optional cloud backup uses Google Drive's secure infrastructure. The App does not maintain any external servers or databases.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google Sign-In: For account authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Drive: For optional cloud backup. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data. To delete cloud backup data, you can do so through your Google Drive settings or by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
