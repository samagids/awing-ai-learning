### FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters)

```
Learn Awing: The Voice of Cameroon's Heritage

Discover the Awing language (azo), a Grassfields Bantu language spoken by approximately 19,000 people in the Mezam division, North West Province, Cameroon. As fewer young people learn their heritage language each year, Awing AI Learning brings interactive, AI-powered education to kids and beginners worldwide.

WHAT IS AWING?
Awing is a tonal African language with a rich cultural history. This app teaches you its unique sounds, writing system, and grammar through engaging lessons designed specifically for children and language learners.

COMPLETE LANGUAGE LEARNING JOURNEY

★ Beginner Level (Master the Fundamentals)
• Learn the 31-letter Awing alphabet with clear pronunciation
• Master 1,700+ vocabulary words across 9 categories
• Discover how tone changes meaning (High, Mid, Low, Rising, Falling)
• Practice pronunciation with real-time feedback
• Enjoy stories and greetings in Awing
• Take interactive quizzes and earn rewards

★ Medium Level (Build Fluency)
• Study consonant clusters: prenasalized (mb, nd), palatalized (ty, ky), labialized (tw, kw)
• Master noun classes and singular/plural rules
• Learn sentence construction with real Awing examples
• Practice writing rules and orthography
• Understand vowel systems and syllable types
• Learn verb suffixes and grammatical patterns

★ Expert Level (Advanced Mastery)
• Master advanced tone identification in complex words
• Study elision rules and long/short word forms
• Explore consonant allophonic variations
• Practice authentic Awing conversations
• Take comprehensive challenge quizzes
• Understand sound changes and phonological rules

KEY FEATURES

✓ 6 Character Voices - Meet boy, girl, young man, young woman, man, and woman characters who guide your learning at each level with distinct pronunciation

✓ AI-Powered Lessons - Content verified against official Awing language sources (Awing Orthography Guide, Awing English Dictionary, Awing Phonology Sketch)

✓ 1,700+ Vocabulary Words - Organized by category: body parts, animals, actions, food, family, descriptive words, and more

✓ Spaced Repetition System - Smart review algorithm reminds you exactly when you're about to forget words (Leitner 5-box system)

✓ Pronunciation Practice - Record yourself speaking Awing and receive instant feedback with scoring

✓ Gamification - Earn XP, unlock levels, collect achievement badges, build daily streaks, and track your progress

✓ Stories & Dialogues - Read and listen to authentic Awing stories and conversations with word-by-word breakdowns

✓ Exam Mode - Teachers can create custom quizzes with multiple question types (translate, tone identification, spelling, category matching)

✓ Dark Mode - Learn comfortably in any lighting condition

✓ Offline-First - Download once, learn anywhere. No internet required after initial install.

✓ Privacy-Focused - Minimal data collection. Optional anonymous analytics. No ads, no tracking, no in-app purchases.

✓ Kid-Friendly Design - Bright colors, large buttons, encouraging feedback, and age-appropriate content

✓ Contribution System - Help improve the app by suggesting corrections and new vocabulary (developer reviews and approves all contributions)

DATA & SOURCES

All lesson content is sourced from official linguistic and cultural references:
• Awing Orthography 2005 - The official orthography guide
• Awing English Dictionary - 3,098+ entries compiled by Alomofor Christian, CABTAL (2007)
• A Phonological Sketch of Awing - Detailed phonological research by Bianca van den Berg, SIL Cameroon (2009)

PERFECT FOR

• Children learning their heritage language
• Linguists and language enthusiasts studying African languages
• Teachers introducing endangered language documentation
• Anyone curious about linguistic diversity and language preservation
• Gamers who love learning through interactive challenges

MISSION

Awing AI Learning celebrates linguistic diversity and helps preserve an endangered language by making it accessible to the next generation. Language preservation starts with accessibility.

7. **Target Audience:** Primarily children ages 5-18 and adults learning the Awing language. All content is age-appropriate and educational.
