Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Awing AI Learning, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Awing AI Learning is designed to be offline-first, meaning you can use the app to learn the Awing language without ever needing to create an account or connect to the internet. All core learning features work without any data collection.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Voluntarily

Google Sign-In (Optional): If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information. This is used only to:

  • Create and manage your user profile within the app
  • Sync your learning progress and settings across multiple devices using Firebase Firestore
  • Enable the optional teacher exam functionality

Signing in with Google is completely optional. The app functions fully offline without it.

2. Information Collected Automatically

Anonymous Analytics (Optional): If you enable anonymous usage analytics (opt-in setting), we collect:

Activity Events

• Lessons opened
• Quiz attempts and scores
• Features used

Device Info

• Unique anonymous device ID
• App version
• Timestamp of events

This data is completely anonymous — no personal identifying information is collected. You can opt out of analytics at any time in Settings > Feedback.

3. Contribution System Data

If you use the Contribute feature to suggest corrections or new vocabulary:

  • Your submission (text and optional audio) is stored locally on your device
  • When you share a contribution, it is sent (via email, WhatsApp, or other platform) to the developer for review
  • The developer reviews, approves, or rejects contributions before applying them to future app versions
  • You retain full control over sharing your contributions

Cloud Data Storage (Firebase Firestore)

How We Use Your Data

If you sign in with Google, your learning progress and settings are stored in Firebase Firestore (Google's cloud database) to enable:

  • Cross-device sync: Your progress automatically syncs when you use the app on different devices
  • Progress backup: Your learning history, quizzes, achievements, and preferences are backed up
  • Exam functionality: Teachers can create and monitor exams for students

Data Security

Firebase Firestore data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Access is controlled by Firebase security rules that ensure:

  • Only you can read and write your own data
  • Data is never visible to other users
  • Google staff cannot access your personal data

Data Retention

Your cloud data persists as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of all cloud data at any time by contacting samagids@gmail.com.

Third-Party Services

Google Services

  • Google Sign-In: Used for optional authentication
  • Firebase Firestore: Used for optional progress sync across devices
  • Google Sheets: Developers may log anonymous analytics events to a spreadsheet (opt-in)

Google's use of your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Microsoft Edge TTS

Awing AI Learning uses Microsoft Edge TTS for text-to-speech pronunciation audio. Edge TTS does not collect personal data — it processes text locally without storing or logging your requests.

Third-Party Libraries

The app uses open-source libraries listed in pubspec.yaml. These libraries do not collect personal data. For more information, consult each library's privacy documentation.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Awing AI Learning does NOT:
  • Collect location data
  • Collect device identifiers (IMEI, phone number, etc.)
  • Collect contacts or calendar information
  • Use cookies or tracking pixels
  • Display third-party advertisements
  • Perform fingerprinting or device profiling
  • Share personal data with advertisers, marketers, or data brokers
  • Sell or monetize your data in any way

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Awing AI Learning is designed for children ages 5 and up. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other child safety regulations:

Our Commitments to Children's Safety

  • No targeted advertising: The app contains no ads, tracking, or behavioral profiling
  • Age-appropriate content: All educational content is reviewed for age-appropriateness
  • Minimal data collection: We collect only essential data needed for the app to function, and analytics are completely anonymous
  • Parental oversight: Parents/guardians can review and manage their child's account and data
  • No social features: The app does not include social media integration, messaging, or user-to-user communication
  • No in-app purchases: The app is completely free with no premium features or monetization

Parental Consent

If a child under 13 uses the app with Google Sign-In, we obtain verifiable parental consent through Google's age-appropriate sign-in flow. Parents can:

  • Review their child's learning progress
  • Manage cloud sync and data storage settings
  • Request deletion of their child's data

Your Privacy Rights

Access Your Data

You can access your learning progress and settings within the app at any time. If you want a copy of your cloud data, contact samagids@gmail.com.

Update or Correct Data

You can update your profile information and preferences within the app's Settings screen.

Delete Your Data

You have the right to request deletion of all your personal data, including:

  • Your Google account link and profile
  • All cloud-stored learning progress
  • Analytics events associated with your device

To request data deletion, contact samagids@gmail.com with your request.

Opt Out of Analytics

You can disable anonymous analytics at any time via Settings > Feedback > toggle "Share anonymous usage data".

Export Your Data

The app includes a backup/restore feature that lets you export your learning progress. Contact the developer for additional export options.

Data Security

How We Protect Your Information

  • Encryption in transit: All data sent to/from Firebase uses HTTPS encryption
  • Encryption at rest: Cloud data is encrypted by Google's infrastructure
  • Offline-first design: Most app data stays on your device and is never transmitted
  • Secure authentication: Google Sign-In uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0
  • No insecure storage: Passwords and sensitive tokens are never stored unencrypted

What We Cannot Guarantee

While we implement best practices, no method of data transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to:

  • Use strong passwords for your Google account
  • Keep your device's operating system and apps updated
  • Be cautious when using the app on public Wi-Fi networks

International Data Transfer

If you use Firebase cloud sync, your data may be transferred, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries where Google operates data centers. By using Awing AI Learning, you consent to such transfers. These transfers are protected by standard contractual clauses and Google's security commitments.
